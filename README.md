## About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at The University of Tokyo, working with [Prof. Yoichi Sato](http://www.hci.iis.u-tokyo.ac.jp/~ysato/).

My research interests lie in:
- **Egocentric Vision** — understanding the world from a first-person perspective
- **Representation Learning** — learning transferable and generalizable visual features for video understanding
- **Human Motion Understanding / Forecasting / Generation** — recognizing and modeling human actions/motions
